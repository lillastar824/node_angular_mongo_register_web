FAQs
Q: What can I do with my @sign?
@: Nothing yet, but soon we will be announcing applications that will work with your @sign. If you didn’t check the box to sign up for notifications of new applications, please do that here. Of course, to be cool and ahead of the wave, you can sign all your email and messages with it and have it put on your clothes!!!
Q: What types of applications are in the works?
@: Find me: The first app will let you enter public information and display it in a nice format and we will have a great one for influencers which will include their offers!.

Buzz: We are working on a peer-to-peer contacts app so that the only contact that you will ever have to keep up-to-date is your own! Everyone will update their own contact datas for you. You will have the ability to store all your different personas in this and share as appropriate.

Julz: We are also working on the Julz Vault App

Add name: We are also working on a file-sharing app - think Air Drop, but for all devices, and all locations.

Have an idea for an app that you would like to see? Let us know!
Q: How are these apps different from what is available today?
@: Well first of all they will use your @sign, so signing up will be a breeze, secondly they will respect your privacy, You can be guaranteed that these apps won’t spy on you. We want to make these apps fun as well. Have an app that you would like to see? Let us know.
Q: Will you let me know when new apps are available?
@: Yes! Once you sign up for your @sign you will see a checkbox for allowing us to notify you when we have new apps. Please make sure that box is checked!
Q: What do I have to do to renew my @sign?
@: If you have a free @sign, nothing. If you have a paid for sign, you will need to renew it each year. We want to make sure that you are using your @sign, and if not, make sure it is available to someone else. The renewal price is $10/year.
Q: Can I transfer/sell my @sign to someone else?
@: Yes, we will soon have a way for you to do that. If you need to do that immediately, please email us at transfer@atsign.com. In addition, as the value of @custom signs increases you will be able to charge for a special @sign if you own it.
Q: If someone knows my @sign, what information will they see about me?
@: If someone does a look up on your @sign, they will see your public information. You control what public information is available and can change and update as you need to – which will automatically update anyones contact information on you through buzz. We will soon have an app that will let you easily set up your public profile.
Q: How much does an @sign cost?
@: We have a free @sign generator. You can have any @sign using that tool for free. You can also buy a custom @sign. Multiple words, or first name/last name are $100. Single words and names are $1000 (because there are so few of these) and three characters are $5000. Please don’t buy a trademarked name, as the owner of that trademark will have the rights to it.
Q: How about my current apps? Will they start using my @sign and protect my privacy?
@: We expect that many of your current apps will transition over time. But their adoption will be slower and will happen most likely when they feel the pressure from their customers.
Q: Will there be apps for me to communicate with the companies I do business with?
@: Our vision is we will have business, government and social apps. As the internet grew from communications to content and then commerce – our vision is that all the 3 Cs will be using @signs in the future.
