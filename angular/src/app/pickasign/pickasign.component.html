Here are some tips for picking your @sign:
  • An @sign is forever, so think of it as your brand. Something that seems funny today, might not in the future.
  • Mix it up. If your first name last name combination is taken, try

  • Use an underscore between your name or initial
  • The shorter the better. A short @sign will fit easily on business cards and anywhere else you want to post it.
