Now for some internet
optimism

In the very near future, the internet will change for the better.
Your information will only belong to you and everyone (including ginormous corporations) will ask your permission. And massive data breaches? Well those will be a thing of the past.

A permission-based internet

The @ protocol will change how people interact on the internet by handing people the keys to their data kingdom (datadom). We put people first, by letting them decide who has permission to talk to them, who has access to their data, what data they can access, and for how long.

An internet for the people

When people and companies have to ask your permission, the world becomes a nicer place.

Contextual personas

People are not single dimensional users as most websites would have you believe! In real life you project different personas to different groups. It is time for you to be yourself, whatever version of you that you would like to present.
