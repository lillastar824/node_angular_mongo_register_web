Business/Marketing Internship App Developer Internship (Dart, Flutter)

Title: Business/Marketing Internship

Dates: Monday, June 8 to Friday, July 31, 2020 (option to continue another month of desired)
Location: Remote
Paid: Yes!

Internship Description

The @ Company is a Silicon Valley Company with an innovation we are calling the IofP (Internet of People). We plan to launch October 2020 and have several exciting projects for the Summer of 2020. We are seeking two college interns or recent college grads who are passionate about our mission, have strong communication skills, and have a strong interest in building an internet for people.

Responsibilities

Responsibilities include: (1) market and consumer research and competitive analysis, (2) coordination of weekly meet-up groups and feedback from customers, (3) preparation of social media launch activities, and (4) the possibility of writing of a white paper that you co-author with one of our founders.

Skills Needed

Skills needed for this internship include: (1) Social media creating and posting, (2) marketing and competitive research writing and reports, and (3) The four C’s (Communication, Critical Thinking, Collaboration, and Creativity). Interested applicants should submit by May 10, 2020: Please send an email to denise@atsign.com (1) Resume and (2) a brief statement of why you would want an @sign and what app you would use an @sign with.
