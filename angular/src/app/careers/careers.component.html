Fall 2020 Internship Incubation Program

We are a Silicon Valley company with an innovation we are calling the Internet of People (IofP). We plan to launch October 2020 and have several exciting projects for the Fall of 2020.

Available Opportunities

Market Development Internship
We are looking for recent college graduates or seniors in any field, who have a strong interest in building a new market in the coming era of people-owned data and surveillance-free advertising. Projects will include market and consumer research and competitive analysis, coordination of a weekly meet-up group with feedback from consumers, preparation of social media launch activities, and business writing, messaging, blogging and vlogging.
App Developer Internship
We are looking for recent college graduates or seniors with a computer science background, Dart and Flutter experience is desired. If you have not used Dart/Flutter but have worked with JavaScript, React, or similar frameworks, this is a great opportunity to learn and gain experience with Flutter. As an App Developer you will be involved in development of an app (Dart, Flutter) on the new @protocol. More on the @protocol.
